How to Use

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box.
  2. Choose the statistic you want to calculate:
    • Count Words - counts the total number of words.
    • Characters (with spaces) - counts characters including spaces.
    • Characters(without spaces) - counts characters excluding spaces.
    • Count Sentences - counts the number of sentences.
    • Count Paragraphs - counts the number of paragraphs.
    • Count Lines - counts the number of text lines.
    • Unique Words - counts distinct words used in the text.
  3. The result will instantly appear in the result box.
  4. Click Copy to Clipboard to copy the result.

About Page

The Word Counter tool is a free online utility that helps you quickly analyze text statistics such as word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, and line count. It also provides advanced features like counting characters with or without spaces and detecting unique words. This tool is commonly used by writers, bloggers, students, editors, and developers to check text length, improve writing, and prepare content for articles, assignments, social media posts, or coding projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a word counter tool?

A word counter tool is an online utility that counts the number of words in a piece of text. It can also provide additional statistics such as character count, sentence count, and paragraph count.

How does a word counter work?

A word counter analyzes the text entered by the user and calculates different statistics like words, characters, sentences, lines, and paragraphs using automated text processing.

What is the difference between characters with spaces and without spaces?

Characters with spaces include every letter, number, symbol, and space in the text, while characters without spaces only count visible characters and ignore spaces.

Why is word count important for writing?

Word count helps writers stay within limits required for essays, blog posts, articles, social media captions, and academic assignments.

What is a unique word counter?

A unique word counter calculates how many different words appear in the text, ignoring repeated words.

Is this word counter tool free to use?

Yes. The tool is completely free and works directly in your browser without requiring installation or login.
