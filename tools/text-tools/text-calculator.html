Frequently Asked Questions

What is a text calculator?

A text calculator is an online tool that analyzes written content and provides useful metrics such as reading time, speaking time, and average word length to help users understand their text better.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is usually estimated based on an average reading speed of around 200–250 words per minute, which helps determine how long it takes a typical reader to finish the text.

How is speaking time calculated?

Speaking time is calculated using the average speaking speed of about 130–160 words per minute, which helps estimate how long it would take to present the text verbally.

Why is average word length useful?

Average word length helps measure the complexity of a text. Shorter words generally make content easier to read, while longer words may indicate more complex language.

Who can use this text calculator?

This tool is useful for writers, bloggers, students, teachers, marketers, and public speakers who want to analyze the structure and delivery time of their written content.

Is this text calculator free?

Yes, this text calculator is completely free and works directly in your browser without requiring any downloads or registration.
