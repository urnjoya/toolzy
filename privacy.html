Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Overview

At abcroot, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online tools platform.

Important: All tools on abcroot operate 100% client-side. Your data is processed entirely in your web browser and never sent to our servers.

Information We Collect

Data Processed Locally

When you use our tools, any text, data, or files you input are processed entirely within your browser using JavaScript. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Is not stored by us in any form
  • Is cleared when you close or refresh the page

Cookies

abcroot does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We may use essential cookies only for:

  • Saving your preferences (e.g., theme selection)
  • Essential site functionality

No third-party tracking cookies are used on our platform.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Advertising: We may display advertisements from third-party networks. These networks may use cookies or similar technologies.

We do not share your personal information.

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's minimal risk of data breaches. Our client-side approach ensures:

  • Your sensitive information stays on your device
  • No databases to hack or leak
  • Complete privacy for your data

Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request information about data we collect
  • Request deletion of any data we hold
  • Opt-out of non-essential cookies
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

To exercise these rights, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
